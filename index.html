What have I done before?

Albany ePay Payment Software


UI/UX design and frontend development for a software company. The client required a bespoke frontend to explain their payment software products, using the latest web technologies. The frontend was developed using Backbone.js as a framework and HTML5/CSS3.

Albany Software Ltd


Web design and development for a software company. The client required an extensive site to explain their services, showcase products and market new business. The website was integrated with a CMS (Umbraco).

Van Der Plank Scaffolding


Briefed to design and develop a website for a scaffolding services company based in Hampshire. The client desired an online presence to showcase work and market their services to a larger audience. We also provided our photography services to capture their previous projects.

www.vanderplank-scaffolding.com

MD Landscaping


Web design and development for a garden landscaping company, with a small budget. The client required a site to explain their services, showcase previous work and market the business. The task was to present large amounts of information over one web page without cluttering.

www.mdlandscaping.co.uk

Royal Navy MWS Intranet


Web maintenance, design and development of the main Marine Warfare School (MWS) Intranet. We revamped the tired introductory page by redesigning banners and navigation buttons whilst working within a rigid template. A new page was created for news items, which the PR department used to promote past and present events.

Emery Building & Carpentry


Web design and development for a construction and joinery company. The client brief was to design and created an elegant and classic site offering their services, a gallery of work and attracting new business.

www.emerybuildingcarpentry.com

Emery Bespoke Classics


Web design and development for a bespoke kitchen, bathroom and bedroom maker. The client wanted a simple, clean and modern web presence, displaying their services and marketing a new business. We also provided our photography services to capture their previous projects.
